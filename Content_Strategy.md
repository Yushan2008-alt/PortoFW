## 1. Tone of Voice

### Voice Principles

| Trait | Description | Do | Don't |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Professional** | Sounds like an agency, not a freelancer side-hustle | "Forza Studio membangun..." | "Gue dan partner gue bikin..." |
| **Convincing** | Backs claims with numbers/proof | "Conversion rate naik 47%" | "Websitenya bagus banget" |
| **Results-oriented** | Frames everything around business outcomes | "Website yang menggerakkan bisnis" | "Website yang keren" |
| **Warm, not corporate-stiff** | Indonesian SME owners respond to approachable tone | "Ceritakan kebutuhan bisnismu" | "Silakan mengisi formulir berikut" |
| **Confident, not arrogant** | States capability without overselling | "Kami bangun produk yang terbukti bekerja" | "Kami adalah yang terbaik di Indonesia" |

### Language Rules

```
Body copy:        Bahasa Indonesia (primary)
UI labels/CTAs:    English OK if it reads naturally (e.g. "Explore →")
Section headers:   Mix OK (e.g. "FAQ", "Testimonials" as eyebrow labels)
Numbers/metrics:   Always in the format users recognize —
                   "Rp 5 juta" not "Rp 5,000,000"
                   "47%" not "47 percent"
Avoid:             Heavy jargon ("synergize", "leverage", "paradigm")
                   Overly casual slang in form/CTA copy
```

### 2.10 Testimonial Format Template

Following eloqwnt's structure: **long quote → name → title → company → LinkedIn link**

```
Template:
─────────────────────────────────
"{2-5 sentence quote describing the experience and result}"

{Name}
{Title} at {Company}
[LinkedIn icon →]
─────────────────────────────────
```

**Quote-writing guideline (for real testimonials to be collected):**
A strong testimonial quote should answer 3 things implicitly:
1. What was the problem/need before working with Forza Studio?
2. What was the experience working together like?
3. What was the measurable or felt result after?

**Example structure (placeholder, to be replaced with real client quotes):**
```
"Forza Studio membangun website bisnis kami dari nol. Hasilnya melebihi
ekspektasi — desainnya profesional dan loading-nya sangat cepat. Dalam
2 minggu setelah launch, kami sudah mendapat 3 inquiries dari website."

[Client Name]
Founder at [Company Name]
```
